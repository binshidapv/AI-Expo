AIENI Committees

Meet the dedicated teams behind the First International Conference of the Emirates Identity and Citizenship Academy.

The Higher Preparatory Committee

AIENI Scientific Committee

The AIENI Scientific Committee is responsible for reviewing and evaluating all submitted abstracts and research papers. This distinguished panel of experts ensures the highest academic standards and scientific rigor for the conference.

The committee comprises leading researchers and academics from renowned institutions, specializing in artificial intelligence, national identity, cybersecurity, and related fields.

  • ✓ Abstract Review & Evaluation
  • ✓ Research Quality Assurance
  • ✓ Academic Program Development
  • ✓ Speaker Selection & Coordination

AIENI Organising Committee

The AIENI Organising Committee oversees all logistical and operational aspects of the conference. From venue management to attendee registration, this team ensures a seamless and memorable experience for all participants.

Working closely with ADNEC Centre Abu Dhabi and various stakeholders, the committee coordinates every detail to deliver a world-class international conference.

  • ✓ Event Planning & Logistics
  • ✓ Registration Management
  • ✓ Venue Coordination
  • ✓ Attendee Services & Support

AIENI Media Committee

The AIENI Media Committee manages all communications, public relations, and media coverage for the conference. This team ensures maximum visibility and engagement across all platforms.

From social media campaigns to press releases and live coverage, the Media Committee brings the conference to a global audience and documents this landmark event.

  • ✓ Media Relations & Press Coverage
  • ✓ Social Media Management
  • ✓ Photography & Videography
  • ✓ Content Creation & Marketing
