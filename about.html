Abu Dhabi

A diverse and globally connected emirate, Abu Dhabi is the federal capital of the United Arab Emirates (UAE). Home to more than 200 nationalities, Abu Dhabi is a growing global financial centre, a thriving innovation hub, a leading energy producer, and a world-class tourist destination and cultural hub.

Abu Dhabi is the largest emirate in the UAE and is divided into three regions: Abu Dhabi City, Al Ain and Al Dhafra.

The Emirates Academy of Identity and Citizenship (EAIC)

The Emirates Academy of Identity and Citizenship (EAIC), was first established as Emirates Institute for Citizenship, Residence and Ports in 2008 by Federal Law issued by the Ministers Cabinet regulation No (37) of 2008 within the organizational structure of the General Directorate of Citizenship, Residence & Ports of the Ministry of Interior.

Its mission was to act as a specialized vocational training in the field of citizenship, residence and ports security affairs. It offered and continued to offer unique and outstanding non-degree vocational training.

In 2012, it gained the status of higher education institution, when it received Initial Licensure from the Ministry of Education through CAA procedures. EAIC diploma and bachelor programs in Citizenship, Residence & Ports were initially accredited by the CAA in 2014.

"The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Enhancing National Identity"

Date: 30–31 March 2026 | Abu Dhabi

In the era of the Fourth Industrial Revolution and the rapid transformations in the digital world, the importance of artificial intelligence becomes evident as a powerful tool capable of enhancing national identity in unprecedented innovative ways.

The conference brings together a distinguished group of researchers and scientists in the field of AI innovation to discuss the latest innovations and scientific research that contribute to strengthening digital identity and reflecting the national values of the state.

The conference and exhibition aim to enhance the academic and scientific reputation of the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs, and Port Security. They provide a platform to review and showcase the latest innovations and modern technologies in the field of artificial intelligence, exploring how these advancements can be integrated to improve services.
