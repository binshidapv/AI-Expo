The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Enhancing National Identity

Under the generous patronage of His Excellency the Chairman of the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs & Port Security, the Emirates Academy for Identity and Citizenship is honored to organize its first international conference entitled: "The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Enhancing National Identity."

The convening of this conference comes at an important historical stage, where the vision of the future meets the legacy of the past, affirming the United Arab Emirates' deep commitment to preserving its national identity and strengthening its societal values—particularly through the employment of the latest scientific and technological innovations in the field of artificial intelligence, in accordance with legal and regulatory frameworks, and in embodiment of the wise leadership's vision for a developed, cohesive society that recognizes its active role in shaping the future.

This conference serves as a vital international platform for exchanging expertise and knowledge, discussing global best practices in the field of artificial intelligence, and showcasing pioneering experiences that can be leveraged to enhance national identity and build a more cohesive and innovative society. It also presents an opportunity to reflect on the immense future potential of this technology and harness it in ways that serve the individual, the community, and the nation, fostering optimism in a progressive present and a promising future.

Through this conference, the Emirates Academy for Identity and Citizenship reaffirms its unwavering commitment to supporting scientific research, encouraging innovation, and promoting national awareness, to ensure that the United Arab Emirates remains a global model in employing artificial intelligence to serve the individual, the community, and the state—reflecting the strength of national will and the aspiration for a civilized future.

Conference Objectives

  • • To establish a strategic understanding of the interactive relationship between artificial intelligence and national identity in light of global digital transformation.
  • • To analyze the legislative and legal frameworks that regulate the use of artificial intelligence technologies and their impact on preserving cultural identity and national values.
  • • To review pioneering international experiences and practices in employing artificial intelligence to protect national identity, both tangible and intangible heritage.
  • • To formulate future visions that contribute to developing national policies ensuring the safe, responsible, and effective use of artificial intelligence technologies.
  • • To strengthen international research partnerships among universities, specialized centers, and decision-making bodies to support national innovation related to identity.
